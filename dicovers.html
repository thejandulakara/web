Types of Dinosaurs

Tyrannosaurus Rex

Discovered in 1902, the T-Rex skeleton is one of the most complete dinosaur fossils ever found. It was a large carnivorous dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period.

Velociraptor

First discovered in 1923, recent findings suggest Velociraptors had feathers. They were small, agile predators that lived during the Late Cretaceous period.

Triceratops

Discovered in 1887, new Triceratops fossils continue to be found, providing insights into their behavior. They were herbivorous dinosaurs with three horns.

Stegosaurus

Discovered in 1877, the Stegosaurus is known for its distinctive back plates and spiked tail. It lived during the Late Jurassic period.

Brachiosaurus

Discovered in 1903, the Brachiosaurus was one of the tallest and largest dinosaurs. It was a herbivore that lived during the Late Jurassic period.

Ankylosaurus

Discovered in 1908, the Ankylosaurus was heavily armored with a club-like tail. It lived during the Late Cretaceous period.
